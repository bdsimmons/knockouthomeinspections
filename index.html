About Knockout

Nathan Lippincott has over 15 years of experience in the construction industry. Born into a family of builders, he grew up on job sites and learned the ins and outs of the trade at a very early age. He spent the majority of his adult years as a supervisor framing houses for Southern Home Framing. He also built many homes from the ground up and knows the process well.

Nathan is now a home inspector with certifications from the International Association of Certified Home Inspectors, Exterior Insulation and Finish Systems (EIFS), and the American Home Inspectors Training Institute.

Superior customer service and satisfaction are at the top of Nathan's priorities as a home inspector. Call him for a quote. You won't be disappointed!

Full Service Inspections

As an InterNACHI member Knockout Home Inspections must adhere to InterNACHI’s Standards of Practice. This means that Knockout will attempt to inspect all of the following (when accessible):

  • Roof, vents flashings, and trim
  • Gutters and downspouts
  • Skylight, chimney, etc.
  • Decks, stoops, porches, walkways, and railings
  • Eaves, soffit and fascia
  • Grading and drainage
  • Basement, foundation, and crawlspace
  • Heating systems
  • Cooling systems
  • Water heating system
  • Interior plumbing fixtures and faucets
  • Drainage sump pumps with accessible floats
  • Electrical service line and meter box
  • Main disconnect and service line and meter box
  • Electrical panels, breakers, and fuses
  • Grounding and bonding
  • Fireplace damper door and hearth
  • Insulation and ventilation
  • Garage doors, safety sensors, and openers
